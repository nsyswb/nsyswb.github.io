About The Band

North Shore Youth Symphonic Wind Band is a multi-award winning concert band based on Auckland's North Shore. The band's primary focus is on providing an environment in which young brass, woodwind and percussion musicians can learn and excel at fun and challenging music, alongside experienced conductors and talented fellow members. This is done in a social environment in which members are able to meet and network with other musicians.
